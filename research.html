Ongoing Research

I am currently researching Change Management for AI-enabled Medical Devices that is aligned to current regulatory and standard requirements. I aim to develop a Change Management web application that provides State of the Art regulatory requirements and guidance for ensuring safe and effective devices are developed throughout the full life cycle.
